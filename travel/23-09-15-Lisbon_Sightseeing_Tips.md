Certainly! Here are 10 fun things to see and do around Jardim da Cerca da Graça in Lisbon:

1. **Jardim da Cerca da Graça**: Start with the garden itself. It's a lovely place to relax and enjoy the view of Lisbon.

2. **Miradouro da Senhora do Monte**: This nearby viewpoint offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city. It's especially beautiful during sunset.

3. **Feira da Ladra**: If you visit on a Tuesday or Saturday, you can explore this famous flea market. You might find unique items, antiques, and vintage treasures.

4. **Graça Church**: Visit the Igreja da Graça, a historic church with beautiful azulejo tilework and a tranquil atmosphere.

5. **São Jorge Castle**: Just a short walk away, São Jorge Castle is an iconic Lisbon landmark with centuries of history. Explore its walls and enjoy the stunning views.

6. **Alfama District**: Stroll through the charming Alfama neighborhood, known for its narrow streets, colorful houses, and Fado music.

7. **Lisbon Cathedral**: Visit the Sé de Lisboa, the city's oldest church, and explore its Gothic architecture.

8. **Museu do Fado**: If you're interested in Fado music, this museum provides insights into its history and significance in Portugal.

9. **Lisbon's Trams**: Take a ride on the historic Tram 28, which passes through Graça and offers a unique way to see the city.

10. **Taste Portuguese Cuisine**: Explore local cafes and restaurants to savor delicious Portuguese dishes like pastéis de nata, bacalhau, and pastel de bacalhau.

Enjoy your time in Lisbon! If you need any more information or specific recommendations, feel free to ask.
